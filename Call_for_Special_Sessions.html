International Conference on Intelligent Computing

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology(A),Hyderabad, March 24th & 25th, 2023


Academicians and acclaimed researchers are invited to organize Special Sessions on emerging thematic topics in optimization techniques. The special sessions should address a specific theme. Minimum 8 papers are required to be registered and presented under a special session. Proposers are requested to submit the filled in Special Session Proposal form available at Downloads section. Special Session proposers will be the Session Chair(s) in the respective sessions. A certificate of appreciation along with certificate of session chairing will be provided to the Special Session proposers.

Special session proposal submission deadline: 15th February, 2023
