International Conference on Intelligent Computing

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (A), Hyderabad, September 8th & 9th, 2023

 

The primary goal of the First International Conference on Intelligent Computing (ICIS)-2023 is to stimulate research, development, and implementation of cutting-edge intelligent computing techniques by offering a dynamic and productive forum across numerous disciplines. An additional goal of ICIS is to raise industry awareness of cutting-edge intelligent computing techniques among scientists, academics, engineers, and students from around the globe as well as the industry to present and exchange new perspectives and application experiences for resolving in-the-moment issues.

We cordially invite you to submit your research papers on the lisited conference tracks.

Authors are invited to submit complete unpublished papers, which are not under review in any other conference or journal, to https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ICRCPM2023.

Position papers are also welcome and should be clearly marked as such.
