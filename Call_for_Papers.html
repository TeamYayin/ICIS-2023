International Conference on Intelligent Computing

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (A), Hyderabad, September 8th & 9th, 2023

Tracks and Themes


Prospective authors are requested to submit high quality original and unpublished full research papers in the following areas for real world applications for presentation during the conference. Any theoretical, empirical, or experimental work on optimization models and related mathematics, solution methods, and systems is encouraged.
